Universal Design for Learning Application Project

EDU 250

UDL Lesson Plan

Goal

The student will use iterative design process to create a program. Identify the goal and objectives of the program. (MSCSE-9.AP.2a)

Lesson Description

The teacher will open the class demonstrating the process of designing a computer program to the entire group. Students will spend time brainstorming ideas about a computer program or game to create, and then fill out the first page of a game design document template. Students will then practice further refining their ideas. At the end of the class, students are given the opportunity to share their ideas with the class.

Multiple Means of Engagement

Recruiting Students' Interests Students will have the opportunity to choose the theme and type of their program or game. During the brainstorming phase, students will have the option to work in groups, with the teacher, or independently. The design template for their program will be available in both paper and digital formats.
Sustaining Effort and Persistence This lesson is solely dedicated to the brainstorming and planning phase so as to not overwhelm students with the task of designing a program. The teacher will encourage the students' creativity, but provide gentle reminders to keep it simple in scope.
